Terms & Conditions – Xchange BD

1. Service Description

Xchange BD is a manual money transfer assistant app that helps users send money between popular Bangladeshi mobile banking services like bKash, Nagad, and Rocket.

Disclaimer: This is not an official app of bKash, Nagad, or Rocket. All transactions are manually processed by our admin team based on the user's submitted orders.

2. Account Registration & Data Collection

To use our service:

  • Users must log in with an email address.
  • Users must provide the account number for the recipient during order placement.
  • We also collect:
    • Order timestamp (from the device)
    • Payment method
    • Transaction history details

All data is securely stored and only used to operate the service.

3. Transfer Order Rules

  • Orders are accepted only after payment is completed.
  • No fake or unpaid orders are possible due to system checks.
  • Orders are processed manually by admins from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, usually within 10–20 minutes.
  • Orders placed outside service hours are fulfilled the next day starting at 10:00 AM.
  • If the user provides an incorrect account number, they can contact support before transfer is made to correct it.
  • No refunds are provided after payment.

4. Withdrawal System

  • Free Withdrawals: Processed within 24 hours.
  • Paid Withdrawals: Treated as transfer orders and processed within 10–20 minutes during service hours.
  • Referral earnings can only be withdrawn through this system.

5. Service Availability

When users tap the “Transfer” button, the app checks if the service is currently available.
If available – users can proceed with payment.
If not available – a dialog appears showing that the service is temporarily closed.
